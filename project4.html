Core Gameplay

Built for Adrenaline

Every mechanic designed to push players to the edge of their reflexes

01
🚗

Wrong Lane Racing

Drive head-on against oncoming traffic at increasing speed. Swipe to dodge. One mistake ends the run.

02
⚡

Turbo Jump System

Swipe up to launch into the air and soar over enemy cars. Limited charges — use them wisely or earn more by watching ads.

03
🛡️

Shield Power-Up

Collect shield pickups on the road for crash immunity. Buy a shop shield before the game starts for extended protection.

04
🪙

Coin Economy

Gold coins spawn on the road during play. Collect them to spend in the in-game shop on power-ups between rounds.

05
❤️

Lifeline System

Crash, but not dead yet — watch a rewarded ad to continue from where you left off. Multiple lifelines per game.

06
📈

Progressive Difficulty

Speed ramps up every second. New enemy cars join traffic as your score climbs. Up to 6 enemies on road at once.

Dodge or Die

Controls

How to Play

Simple to learn. Impossible to master.

01
👆

Tap Start

Hit START PLAY on the home screen. Visit the shop first if you have coins to spend.

02
↔️

Swipe to Dodge

Drag your finger left or right to steer the car and avoid incoming traffic.

03
⬆️

Swipe Up to Jump

Swipe upward or tap the JUMP button to launch over enemies. 3 charges per game.

04
🏆

Survive & Score

Keep dodging. Beat your best score. Spend coins in the shop for the next run.

Arsenal

Power-Ups & Items

Six ways to gain the edge on the wrong lane

🛡️

Road Shield

Pick up from the road for crash immunity. Absorbs hits then disappears.

🪙

Gold Coins

Spawned along the road. Collect to build your shop currency for future runs.

⚡

Jump Boost

Launch your car into the air over enemies. 3 base charges, refillable via ads or shop.

💎

Shop Shield (8 sec)

Buy before the game for 8 seconds of continuous protection at game start. Costs 80 coins.

Lifeline Continue

Watch a rewarded ad after crashing to revive and continue from the same score.

🎯

+3 Shop Jumps

Buy an extra jump pack for 50 coins. Activates at the start of the next game.

Under the Hood

Tech Stack

Built entirely with native Android — no game engine needed

☕

Java

Core language — all game logic written in pure Java

🎨

Canvas API

Custom 2D rendering via Android Canvas & Paint — no Unity, no LibGDX

🗄️

SharedPreferences

Persistent best score, coin balance, music settings, equipped items

📢

Google AdMob

Rewarded ads for jumps, lifelines, and coin rewards via GDPR-compliant UMP
