Instead of manually calculating or remembering expenses, this app allows users to record income and expenses in one place and clearly understand their financial situation. The goal of BudgetBuddy is to promote better financial habits, help users control spending, and make smarter financial decisions through a clean and user-friendly interface.

App Overview

BudgetBuddy works as a personal finance tracker where users can easily log their daily income and expenses. The app provides a simple dashboard showing financial summaries, helping users quickly understand where their money is going. With an intuitive design and smooth navigation, users can manage their budget without any complexity.

Key Features

  • Income & Expense Tracking: Users can easily add and manage their daily income and expenses.
  • Category Management: Expenses and income can be categorized for better financial analysis.
  • Real-Time Balance: The app automatically calculates and shows the current balance.
  • Simple Dashboard: Users can quickly view summaries of their financial activities.
  • Offline Functionality: The app works completely offline using local storage.
  • Lightweight & Fast: Optimized for performance to run smoothly on most Android devices.

How the Application Works

Users can add their income and expense records through a simple form. Each entry is stored locally and categorized properly. The app then processes this data and displays a summary, including total income, total expenses, and current balance. This allows users to monitor their financial habits and adjust spending accordingly.

Why This App Is Useful

Managing money is important, but many people struggle to keep track of their spending. BudgetBuddy simplifies this by giving users a clear overview of their finances. It helps users avoid overspending, save money, and build better financial discipline by providing accurate and easy-to-understand insights.

Technical Implementation

The application was developed using Java in Android Studio. All financial data is stored locally using SQLite, ensuring fast performance and offline functionality. The app focuses on simplicity, speed, and usability, making it accessible for all types of users.

Technology Stack

  • Java – Core Android development language
  • Android Studio – Development environment
  • SQLite – Local database for storing transactions
  • AdMob Ads – Monetization integration
